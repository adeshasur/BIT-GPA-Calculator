UCSC BIT GPA Calculator for Sri Lankan External Degree Students

This unofficial GPA and Repeat calculator is calibrated for the Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) curriculum of the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC), Sri Lanka. It correctly handles the 1.50 GPA progression rule, grade capping for repeated subjects at C (2.00 GPV), non-GPA Enhancement (EN) courses, and provides instant status validation for DIT, HDIT, and BIT milestones. Reference code and layout are designed for ease of use on both desktop and mobile.
